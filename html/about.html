2022 Pokémon World Championships

Pokémon World Championships are the pinnacle of competitive Pokémon play. Typically occurring in August of each year, the Pokémon World Championships feature the best Pokémon players from around the globe. Players will compete for over a half million dollars in prizes, the title of Pokémon World Champion, and return invitations for the following year's Worlds.
